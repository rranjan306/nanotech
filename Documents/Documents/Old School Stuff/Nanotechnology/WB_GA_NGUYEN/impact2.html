Environmental Impact

Nanotech has also been used to help the environment. Although it hasn't happened yet, scientists think that in the future, nanotechnology may be able to reduce pollution as an effect of manufacturing and remove pollutants from the environment. Recently, a new coating that makes solar panels more efficient has been developed with nanotechnology. Researchers from the Future Chips Constellation (FCC) at Rensselear Polytechnic Institute in New York have found that the new coating can absorb about 96.21% of all the sun exposure it receives, much higher than the 67.4% regular silicon solar panels now receive. The coating is made out of seven layers of nanoscale silicon dioxide and titanium dioxide rods and can absorb all types of sunlight from many different angles.
